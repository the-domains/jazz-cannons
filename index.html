Jimmy Cannon

Singer and Saxophonist Jimmy Cannon Obtaining a post-graduate Diploma in Jazz Performance and Composition from Trinity College of Music and awarded the Archers Scholarship, Jimmy has toured and performed with Sir Tom Jones, The Pasadena Roof Orchestra, The Commitments, The Syd Lawrence Orchestra, Elvis The Ultimate Performance, runs his own swing band The Jazz Cannons, and sings with the English Heritage band Burton Bradstock.
